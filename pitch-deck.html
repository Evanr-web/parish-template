Eparchy Control

"Centralized management. Decentralized ownership."

  • Dashboard: See all parishes at a glance — status, last updated, contact
  • Push announcements to every parish at once — pastoral letters, eparchial events, clergy changes
  • Emergency access: Fix a broken site remotely if needed
  • Template updates roll out automatically — every parish gets improvements
  • Each parish still owns their site — content, images, personality remain theirs

The eparchy gains visibility and coordination. Parishes keep their autonomy.

What We Need From You

"Your blessing. Three parishes. That's it."

  • Identify 2–3 pilot parishes — ideally different sizes and needs
  • A contact person at each parish (doesn't need to be technical)
  • Feedback during pilot — what works, what doesn't, what's missing
  • Eventually: eparchy endorsement for wider rollout

We handle everything else — design, build, hosting, support. The parish just says yes.
